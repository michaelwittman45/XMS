ABOUT | XMS

,

X-Mega-Saw, or XMS, is a forward-thinking platform focused on educating, guiding, and assisting users in building the ultimate custom PC experience. Built by a passionate team of tech enthusiasts, XMS combines deep hardware knowledge, clean design, and expert recommendations to demystify the world of PC components. From beginners to hardcore builders, XMS is here to guide every step of the build.

Our Mission

The mission of XMS is to make PC hardware accessible, understandable, and exciting for everyone. Through detailed guides, component breakdowns, comparisons, and visual content, we help users make informed decisions. Our unique edge comes from the individual hardware specializations within our team, ensuring expert-level insight on every key component of a modern build.

Meet the XMS Team

Each member of the XMS team brings a unique hardware focus, enabling deep, hands-on expertise across the entire PC building ecosystem. Together, we cover all the essential parts of a custom build from power to performance to aesthetics.

  • Jesse – PSU Specialist: Focused on power delivery, efficiency, and safe system operation.
  • Raquuan – GPU Lead: Covers all things graphics, from gaming to rendering performance.
  • Jack – CPU Analyst: Handles processing power, core comparisons, and workload optimization.
  • George – RAM Expert: Ensures memory choices fit system needs and performance goals.
  • Kobe – Motherboard Strategist: Guides compatibility, features, and chipset selection.
  • Wiremu – Storage Architect: Specializes in SSDs, HDDs, speeds, and data reliability.
  • Arthur – Case Designer: Focused on airflow, form factor, and aesthetic presentation.
  • Tupac – RGB Integration: Expert in visual lighting setups, sync systems, and customization.
  • Jamie – Monitor Reviewer: Provides insights on refresh rates, panel types, and screen performance.
  • Matarangi – Peripherals Coordinator: Reviews keyboards and mice, blending performance and feel.

Our Approach

We believe that building a PC is both a technical project and a creative expression. That’s why each section of our website is written with precision and passion by the team member who lives and breathes that component. Our guides are grounded in research, real-world benchmarks, and hands-on experience, making XMS a trustworthy partner in your PC-building journey.

The XMS Vision

Our vision is to evolve XMS into a community hub where education meets inspiration. We aim to integrate comparison tools, interactive builds, video tutorials, and live Q&A sessions to further help users get the most out of their builds. From the inside of your PSU to the glow of your RGB, we want every user to build with confidence and creativity.
